Spinatpfannkuchen

Description

Pancakes filled with spinach, feta cheese, and tomatoes

Ingredients

For the dough
  • 150ml Milk
  • 100ml Mineral water
  • 100g Flour
  • 3 Eggs
  • a bit of Salt
For the filling
  • 1500g Spinach
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 10 Cocktail Tomatoes
  • 150g Feta
  • 8TL Oil
  • a bit of Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. First, for the pancake batter, put the flour in a bowl. Add the eggs, salt, mineral water, and goat's milk, and mix everything together with a whisk.

    Work quickly to avoid lumps. Then cover the bowl and set the batter aside to rest.

  2. Now wash the spinach thoroughly. Change the water several times if necessary until the spinach is free of sand.

  3. Peel the onions and garlic, chop both finely, and sauté in 2 teaspoons of oil. Add the spinach while still wet and sauté over medium heat until the liquid has evaporated.

    Season to taste with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Set aside and allow to cool slightly.

  4. Wash the cocktail tomatoes and cut them in half. Drain the sheep's cheese and cut it into small pieces. Mix both into the spinach.

  5. Heat 1/2 teaspoon of oil in a coated pan and gradually bake 8 pancakes. Alternatively, bake 6 thicker pancakes to make a total of 6 pancakes.

    One serving consists of 2 pancakes.

  6. Top each pancake with one-eighth (one-sixth) of the filling, roll up, and serve immediately.
